Data Analyis EDA (Exploratory Data Analysis)

I've yet to start it (will soon), but the idea is that I take the data of complaints made to the NYPD (data from OpenData NYC), and I plan to filter it by borough, neighborhoods in the borough, demographics in each, income, and try to see if there is any correlation.



The questions I intend to answer are:

  • What is a common trait between all these boroughs? Neighborhoods?
  • What type of complaints are made in each?
  • Are the complaints in correlation to the area?
  • Does Crime Rate have anything to do with this? Crime Rate/Criminal Activity
