About Me

Hey! My name is Jesus Baltazar, and I am an aspiring Front-End/Back-End Developer, Software Engineer, and Data Analyst (Recently got into Data).

I am currently pursuing an Undergraduate Bachelor's degree in Computer Science (BA) at CUNY Hunter College. I am looking to make an impact in the world using technology. Whether it be raising awareness or contributing to projects for the greater good!

My first introduction to the world of coding was in the 6th grade of middle school. I had taken my first "serious" Computer Science class, and my first language that I was ever introduced to was HTML! Since then, I knew I wanted to do something with programming, I just did not know what. I didn't receive much exposure to coding after the 6th grade, until my second year of High school. I learned Python, and its relation to game development. This pushed me into wanting to get into that realm of programming. I tried to download game engines and learn them, but rather opted in for the more basic approach, learning more languages.

When I reached college, I was exposed to the world of code and was comfortable enough to where I wanted to pursue it on my own time as well as in school. At the time of writing this, I have taken one class on Python, and one class on C++, and on my own time, started to learn Javascript, and also practicing HTML and CSS. I intend to keep going on this path and introduce new projects and ideas to this site. I initially entered college with the thought that I would be a Game Developer/Game Designer. The more things I was exposed to, and the more research I did, the more I decided to branch out.

What I plan to do in the future is work somewhere in the area of Web development, but am VERY interested in exploring the areas of Software Engineering, and Data Analysis. Working with HTML/CSS, some JavaScript and other frameworks, but also working with the idea of designing websites also interests me. As I continue to learn more about CSS and perfect my "vision" with designing, this website will be periodically updated with new looks and functionalities.
